Who We Are

The team of Independent Study Abroad works to empower students to fulfill their dreams of pursuing an MBBS education abroad. Our highly skilled education advisers and counselors are committed to assisting students in realizing their full potential via personalized guidance and assistance.

Through world-class education- students will hone their knowledge and expertise in medical science. We strive to make the admissions process as simple and hassle-free as possible. Now that students can focus on what matters: their education and personal development!

What We Offer

Documentation Assistance

Our team assists students in preparing all necessary documents for admission to their desired universities abroad.

Travel Arrangement

We help students with travel arrangements, including flight bookings and visa assistance.

Accommodation

We support finding suitable accommodation near their university, such as student housing or homestays.

Counseling

Our team of experienced counselors offers guidance and support to students throughout the application and admission process and during their stay abroad.

Visa Assistance

We provide visa assistance to all students who enroll through us, taking the stress out of the visa application process.

Admission

We assist students in navigating the admissions process by submitting applications, tracking status, and contacting institutions on their behalf.

Why Choose Us?

We provide comprehensive guidance to study MBBS abroad. Students get various services to achieve their academic and career goals. Our experienced team of education consultants, doctors, and counselors provides personalized guidance and support to students throughout the admission process.
